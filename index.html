Image Mag is a concept design for a new kind of online photo magazine. Massive, responsive images and easy-to-understand navigation are at the heart of the design. Would make a perfect portfolio or projects website.

About the design

Typography

All of the typography is imported from Google Fonts.

Headings: Fjalla One Paragraph: Catamaran

Images

All images are found on the web and cannot be used in a real project. They are only used to simulate a realistic portfolio/project site. The expectation is that you will use original or copyright-free photos when using this as a commercial project.
